Welcome

Welcome to Crochet Along! Here we hope to build a community of crochet enthusiasts. Our dedicated staff is made up of local crochet artists thrilled with spreading the love of their craft. Whether you are a beginner or experienced with crochet, we are excited to offer, helpful tutorials, exciting patterns, useful resources, tools and yarn that meet the needs of your next project.

Our highly qualified staff are here to assist you on your crochet journey. We offer flexible hours and a variety of classes at competative rates. Don't take our word for it, check out what some of our satisfied customers have to say. Don't forget to check out our resources page for any tools or yarn you may need.

Learn to Crochet Now!

Curious about crochet but don’t know where to begin? Why not book a class with one of our experienced staff.

Need Supplies?

Check out our resources page for sites where you can buy crochet hooks, stich markers, yarn, and much more. They have many helpful tools to get you started.
